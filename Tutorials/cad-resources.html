Practical 3D Printing CAD Tips

  • Keep walls at least 2–3 mm thick to avoid breakage
  • Check for floating parts or disconnected objects
  • Use simple shapes at first; avoid tiny details below 1–2 mm
  • Think about how supports may be needed for overhangs
  • Always preview your model in Kiri:Moto
